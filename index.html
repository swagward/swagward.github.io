About Me

Hello! My name is Alex Pannell, I am an indie game developer with a focus on creating performant, well-architected systems in Unity (C#) and Unreal Engine 5 (C++/Blueprints)

My journey started with a love for sandbox games, especially because of their mechanics and procedural generation, leading me to creating Swagaria, a project where I primarily focused on optimising large scale world data and clean architectural patterns.

Beyond sandbox titles, I have experience across the full development stack, from web based frameworks like Phaser to high fidelity environments in UE5. I enjoy the challenge of deconstructing complex technical problems into efficient, readable code, a skill I aim to bring to every project and collaboration.
